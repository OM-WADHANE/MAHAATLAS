Key Maharashtra Property Laws

MahaRERA
Real Estate (Regulation & Development) Act, 2016
Mandatory registration for projects >500 sq.m. or 8+ units. 70% escrow. Delivery timelines legally enforceable.
MOFA 1963
Maharashtra Ownership Flats Act
Promoter obligations, society formation within 4 months, conveyance of land mandatory.
MRTP 1966
Maharashtra Regional & Town Planning Act
FSI zones, DP/TP schemes, NA permissions (Sec. 44/45), Change of Land Use (CLU).
MLR Code
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966
7/12 records, mutations, NA conversion, tribal land restrictions (Sec. 36), agricultural ceiling.
MRC Act
Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999
Urban tenant protections, standard rent, eviction grounds, pagdi/kholi system in Mumbai.
MSA 1958
Maharashtra Stamp Act, 1958
Stamp duty on all instruments. Higher of agreement value or Ready Reckoner (ASR) applies.
